## â“ FAQs

**Q: Do I need to say the words out loud?**
A: No, just perform the ISL signs silently.

**Q: What if I make a mistake in a take?**
A: Just delete it and record again. Only submit good quality takes.

**Q: Can I record on my phone?**
A: Yes! Phone cameras work perfectly. Use the default camera app.

**Q: What if the file is not MP4?**
A: MOV or AVI are also fine. Most phones use MP4 by default.

**Q: Should all my takes be identical?**
A: No! Natural variations are good. Just perform the phrase naturally each time.

**Q: Can I use a webcam instead of phone?**
A: Yes, as long as video quality is good (720p minimum).
